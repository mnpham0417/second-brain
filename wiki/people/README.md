# wiki/people/

Dossiers on people — collaborators, advisors, authors, contacts.

A person page typically has:

- Role / affiliation and how you know them
- Areas of work and current focus
- Notable interactions (with dates and links to the source in `raw/` if applicable)
- Cross-links to relevant projects and concepts

Filenames: kebab-case `firstname-lastname.md` (e.g. `jane-doe.md`). When the same name collides, disambiguate with affiliation: `jane-doe-mit.md`.

Treat these pages as private notes. Stick to professionally-relevant facts and avoid speculation.
