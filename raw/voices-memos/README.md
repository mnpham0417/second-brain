# raw/voices-memos/

Your own voice memos and their transcripts. Use this for thinking-out-loud, walking notes, meeting recordings *you made*, etc.

## What belongs here

- `.m4a` / `.mp3` voice memo files
- Their transcripts as `.md` or `.txt` (preferred — text is what Claude can actually read)
- Optional metadata sidecar: a short header with date, location, and topic

## Where to export from

- **iOS Voice Memos** — share sheet → "Save to Files" → pick the iCloud Obsidian folder, or AirDrop to your Mac.
- **macOS Voice Memos** — drag the recording out of the app window onto Finder.
- **Otter.ai / Whisper / MacWhisper** — feed the `.m4a` in to produce a transcript; save the transcript here next to the audio.
- **Apple Notes / Voice Memos auto-transcript** (iOS 18+) — copy the transcript text into a `.md` file.

Filename convention: `YYYY-MM-DD-HHMM-topic.m4a` and `YYYY-MM-DD-HHMM-topic.md` for the transcript, paired by base name.
