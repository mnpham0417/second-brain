# raw/podcast-transcripts/

Transcripts of podcast episodes — text only. Audio files don't belong here.

## What belongs here

- Auto-generated transcripts from podcast platforms
- Transcripts you generated locally (Whisper, MacWhisper, etc.)
- Hand-edited or human-produced transcripts

## Where to export from

- **Spotify** — many episodes show a transcript pane → copy text, or use the Spotify API.
- **Apple Podcasts** — `Episode → ··· → Transcript` (iOS 17.4+) → select-all and copy.
- **Overcast / Pocket Casts** — share the audio to a transcription tool.
- **Whisper / MacWhisper / whisper.cpp** — transcribe a downloaded `.mp3` locally.
- **Otter.ai, Descript, AssemblyAI** — upload the audio and export the transcript as `.txt` or `.md`.
- **Show notes pages** — many podcasts publish official transcripts on their site.

Filename convention: `YYYY-MM-DD-show-episode-slug.md` with a header containing show name, episode title, host(s), guest(s), and source URL.
