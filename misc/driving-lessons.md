## Steps
- Pre-apply online for a WDL number.
- Study the Washington Driver Guide and pass the knowledge test (32 of 40).
- Get your learner permit.
- Practice — on your own with a qualified licensed driver, and/or with a driving school.
- Pass the road test, then apply for the license.
